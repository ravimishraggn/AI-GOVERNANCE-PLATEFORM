# Phase 5: Leadership (Months 49–60+)

## Scale Target
- Market leadership in private markets AI governance
- Industry standard governance capabilities
- Regulatory recognition

## Primary Objective
Establish the platform as the industry standard for private markets AI governance.

## Key Deliverables

- [ ] Governance language model: specialized AI for policy authoring, risk assessment, compliance analysis
- [ ] Cross-firm governance benchmarking (anonymized peer comparison)
- [ ] Regulatory pre-certification: formal relationships accelerating AI system approvals
- [ ] Federated governance network: metadata sharing across firm boundaries where appropriate
- [ ] Real-time regulatory monitoring with autonomous impact assessment
- [ ] Governance simulation: model policy change impact before implementation
- [ ] AI governance maturity scoring for tenants with improvement roadmaps
- [ ] Integration with AI safety research organizations

## Definition of Success
- Regulators reference the platform as a model for industry governance
- Enterprise customers attribute reduced compliance costs to platform governance
- Governance capabilities generate measurable competitive advantage in enterprise sales
